# cookedindex
**Hormuz Fertilizer Shock — Country Vulnerability Index v4**  
*Exploring the structural impacts of a Strait of Hormuz crisis on global food supply chains.*

A 121-country vulnerability index built from UN Comtrade, FAOSTAT, IMF WEO, World Bank, and USDA data. Asks: if the Strait of Hormuz closes and fertilizer flows are severed, which countries face structural food security failure — and which can absorb the shock?

### Three structural axes
- **Vulnerability** — how directly and severely does the shock reach a country's food system? (Exposure + Transmission sub-scores)
- **Resilience** — what financial and physical buffers exist? (FX reserves, fiscal capacity, food stocks)
- **Adaptability** — what structural escape valves exist? (domestic ammonia, caloric self-sufficiency, supplier diversity, crop flexibility)
