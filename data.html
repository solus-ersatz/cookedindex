Replication note

All files are provided as-is. Data sources span 2021–2025 depending on availability; primary trade data (Comtrade) is 2023. The 2022/2023 baseline is intentional — the index measures structural vulnerability, not the current crisis-distorted state.

Known data gaps: Iran and Iraq flows reconstructed from mirror data (true Hormuz dependence for Pakistan, India, and Afghanistan may be 5–15pp higher than shown). 46 countries have no USDA PSD food reserve data. 37 countries have imputed food expenditure share at dataset median (22.9%). See methodology document for full discussion of limitations.
